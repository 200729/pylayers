CORMORAN

The CORMORAN project, a multi-partner research initiative funded in part by the French National Research Agency (ANR11-INFR010), has been focusing on new forms of cooperation in and between wireless body area networks. Targeted applications include coordinated navigation of groups in buildings or large-scale motion capture for gaming, sports and healthcare.
