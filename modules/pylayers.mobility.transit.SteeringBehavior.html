pylayers.mobility.transit.SteeringBehavior¶

This module is based on Steering Behaviors for Autonomous Characters created by Craig Reynolds and expanded upon by many.

http://www.red3d.com/cwr/steer/ http://www.red3d.com/cwr/papers/1999/gdc99steer.html http://opensteer.sourceforge.net/ http://www.steeringbehaviors.de/

calculate(boid)[source]¶

calculate boid behavior

Parameters:boid

Notes

This behavior is the attraction by the destination POI The desired velocity is oriented toward the destination and scaled with the desired velocity.

If the distance between current position and destination is less than 25cm the boid has got its target point.

pylayers.mobility.transit.SteeringBehavior.queue_steering_mind(boid)[source]¶

Sum of all steering vectors, except Separation in some cases.

The Separation steering vector will be ignored if any prior steering behavior gave a non-zero acceleration, typically Containment.
