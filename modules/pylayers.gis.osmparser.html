cartesian(cart=False, delta=0)[source]¶

convert Latitude/Longitude in cartesian

Parameters:

cart : Boolean

conversion to cartesian

delta : offset

default 0 : in this case the origin corresponds to the lower left point
Returns:

m : Basemap converter

Notes

This method converts latlon coordinates into cartesian x,y coordinates in Cassini projection relatively to specified latlon boundary The basemap objet for back and forth coordinates conversion is returned.

The transformation is centered on the mean of latitude and longitude. The cartesian origin (0,0) correspond to the lower left corner (lonmin,latmin)
