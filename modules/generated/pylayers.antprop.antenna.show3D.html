pylayers.antprop.antenna.show3D¶

show 3D matplotlib diagram

Parameters:

F : ndarray (Nf,Nt,Np)

theta : ndarray (1xNt)

angle

phi : ndarray (1xNp)

angle

theta : ndarray (Nt)

frequency index

Warning

len(theta) must be equal with shape(F)[1] len(phi) must be equal with shape(F)[2]
