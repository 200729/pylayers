pylayers.util.cone¶

Class Cone¶

The following conventions are adopted

  • A cone has an apex which is a point in the plane.
  • A cone has two vectors which define the cone aperture. The order of those two vectors

matters (u) is the starting vector (u) and (v) the ending vector.

The cone region is defined by the convex angular sector going from starting vector \(\mathbf{u}\) to the ending vector \(\mathbf{v}\) rotating in the plane in following the trigonometric rotation convention. The modulus of the cross product between \(\mathbf{u}\) and \(\mathbf{v}\) is positive.

\(\mathbf{u} \times \mathbf{v} = \alpha \mathbf{z} \;\; \textrm{with} \;\;\alpha > 0\)
